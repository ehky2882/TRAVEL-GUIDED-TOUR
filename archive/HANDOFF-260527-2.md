### Naming conventions used

- **Hero image:** `<Tour_Slug>_hero.webp` (matches the URL that was
  already pre-populated in `heroImageURL` for the Task-A tours)
- **Gallery images:** `<Tour_Slug>_2.webp`, `_3.webp`, … numeric. One
  exception — Chapel of Souls kept the existing descriptive `_tiles`
  filename to match the URL the catalog already pointed at.
- **Audio:** `<Tour_Slug>.mp3` (existing convention)

### Merge-conflict resolution

PRs #88, #89, #90 all originally appended after the same Braga tour
(line 2211 of the pre-session Tours.json). After #88 merged, #89 had to
be rebased to append after Expo'98; after #89 merged, #90 had to be
rebased again to append after Piscina. Both rebases were done by
resetting the branch to current `main`, re-applying the new-tour block
to the new tail, then force-pushing — cleaner than resolving the
markers in-place. No business-logic conflict — pure tail-of-array
structural overlap.
