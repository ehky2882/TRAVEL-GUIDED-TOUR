### 2. Two Lellos shipped distinct (owner-confirmed)

Owner pre-confirmed in the session brief that the two Lello entries are
intentionally separate tours. The differentiation lives in the
longDescription content:

- **Tour 3 — Fundação Livraria Lello** is the Foundation's HQ inside the
  **Mosteiro de Leça do Bailio** — a 14th-century fortified Knights
  Hospitaller monastery on the Portuguese Camino, with a Roman temple to
  Jupiter beneath, the 1372 secret royal marriage of Fernando I to
  Leonor Teles, and Álvaro Siza's recent foot-washing fountain in a
  converted storage silo. The Foundation's residency (since 2022) is the
  closing note, not the lead. Filed under city **Matosinhos** (the
  monastery is in Leça do Bailio parish, Matosinhos municipality —
  matches how Casa de Chá da Boa Nova and Piscina das Marés use
  parish-level cities in the same municipality).

- **Tour 4 — Livraria Lello** is the famous Art Nouveau bookstore on
  **Rua das Carmelitas in Porto**, 1906 — Decus in Labore across the
  stained-glass facade, the first reinforced-concrete staircase in
  Portugal (engineer Francisco Xavier Esteves's signature only found
  during restoration a century after he put it there), the Viúva
  Lamego skylight bearing the Lello monogram, and the widely-repeated
  J. K. Rowling Harry Potter inspiration story which Rowling has
  publicly denied. No mention of the Foundation in this entry.

### 3. Briefing correction — Tour 6 is Álvaro Siza, not Souto de Moura

The session brief tagged the Pavilhão Multiusos de Gondomar as "likely
Eduardo Souto de Moura". The transcript explicitly attributes the
building to **Álvaro Siza**: it opens "This is not the famous Seeza
building — it's the other one," then lists Siza's canonical works
(swimming pools at Leça da Palmeira, Serralves museum, FAUP at Campo
Alegre) as prior stops on the same tour series, and cites Siza's
Portuguese Pavilion at Expo'98 ("a sheet of paper resting on two
bricks") as the direct precedent for the 520m² floating concrete canopy
at the Gondomar entrance. Catalog entry attributes to Siza and tags
match. Mentioned in the PR description so the correction is durable.
