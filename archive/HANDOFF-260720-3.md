### What shipped (5 changes)

1. **Follow Requests title → caption font.** Principal-toolbar `Text` in
   `AtlasTypography.caption` (ALL CAPS), matching the other pushed detail
   screens; `.navigationTitle` kept for the accessibility label.
2. **Approve/Decline → colored icon buttons.** Circular buttons — green
   `checkmark` + red `xmark` on tinted-circle backgrounds; VoiceOver labels kept.
3. **Me-tab notification dot.** A small **gold dot** on the **ME** tab icon when
   the signed-in user has pending follow requests on their own maker.
   - `FollowService` gained an observed `ownPendingRequests` +
     `refreshOwnPendingRequests(ownMakerId:)` (seeds synchronously from the
     stale `FollowStateStore` cache, then corrects from the network) +
     `clearOwnPendingRequests()`.
   - `AtlasTabBar` gained `badgedTabs: Set<AtlasTab>` → renders a gold dot
     (ringed in the bar color) on the icon; a11y value "New notifications".
   - `BottomModuleRoot` drives it via `.task(id: ownMakerId)` and reads
     `followService.ownPendingRequests`. Required injecting **`FollowService`
     + `MakerProfileService`** into the bottom-module window (App entry
     `installBottomModule`).
4. **Heart badge on the followers count.** The separate "N follow requests"
   line is gone; a small **gold heart badge** now sits over the followers count
   on the own profile (decorative reminder — the count is the tap target).
5. **Requests page merged INTO the followers list.** No separate requests
   screen anymore. On the own profile, `FollowListView` (kind `.followers`,
   `showsPendingRequests: true`) loads `pendingRequests()` and renders a
   **"PENDING REQUESTS"** section pinned above the **FOLLOWERS**, each with the
   green ✓ / red ✗. Approving inserts that person at the top of the followers
   list in place + calls `onRequestsChange` (refreshes the profile header counts
   + Me-tab dot). **Deleted `Features/Maker/FollowRequestsView.swift`.**
