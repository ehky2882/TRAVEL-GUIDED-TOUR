Here's the story it's keeping. In fifteen seventy-eight Amsterdam changed religion. The Catholic city government was turned out, the Protestants took over, and Catholic worship was banned from public life. But the city attached a very Amsterdam clause: what happened behind your own front door was your own business. Freedom of conscience, not freedom of worship. So the Catholics built churches you couldn't see.
